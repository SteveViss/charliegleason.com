  • Ceres

  • A time sensitive experiment celebrating the lyrics of Ceres' new album.

A designer, developer and internet enthusiast. Also sometimes musician.

When Ceres' released their album Drag It Down On You, they didn't include lyrics. After a bunch of fans got in touch asking for them, we decided to build a time sensitive experiment.

Hand written lyrics for each song would be available for 24 hours, in an easily navigable mobile-first environment, and then they would be gone.

It was a huge success. Also, the album is amazing, so you should go get it.
