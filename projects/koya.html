  • Kōya

  • An interactive, generative piece of art for musician Braille Face.

A designer, developer and internet enthusiast. Also sometimes musician.

Kōya is the debut album from Melbourne-based musician Braille Face.

I was approached by Spirit Level to help out with the interface for an interactive artwork in the browser. Using WebGL and your webcam, if available, Kōya generates an unique environment that changes with every track.

Powered by Ian MacLarty's audio visual toolkit Amulet, and React's create-react-app, Kōya encourages the viewer to literally reach out and interact with the space around them. Inspired by the original artwork by Max Löffler, it's an immersive piece of art that showcases the incredible power of the web.
