  • A collective live music project.

A designer, developer and internet enthusiast. Also sometimes musician.

Moran & Cato came from wanting to share incredible acoustic performances of local musicians online. I, along with my friends, set up a space in a local warehouse, and started recording bands. The first episode launched to a great reception, and the project is ongoing was fun while it lasted.

Also there are a bunch of CSS animation on the site, because CSS animations are great.
