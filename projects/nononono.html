A designer, developer and internet enthusiast. Also sometimes musician.

Nononono started as No More Gifs, a ten minute microsite to try and persude people away from the incredible gif. It was tounge in cheek (I love a gif as much as the next internet enthusiast) and didn't go anywhere. Then I realised there was something in the idea of being able to say no more anything.

Feed it a sub-domain, like kittens or Harlem Shake or hipsters and it will proudly proclaim its derision, using an iconic scene from the Office.

There are some easter eggs in there as well.

It was built with Sinatra and makes pretty extensive use of flex box.
